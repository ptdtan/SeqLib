1 /* Htmshell.h - stuff to make it easier to generate HTML files on
2  * the fly. Typically included with cheapcgi.h in almost any
3  * CGI program.
4  *
5  * To use this generally you should call the function htmShell()
6  * very early inside of main(). You pass htmShell() a routine
7  * which does most of the work of your web server-side applet.
8  *
9  * These routines will throw errors, which are caught by
10  * htmShell, which then returns. For the most part you just
11  * want an error to cause an error message to be printed and
12  * then terminate your CGI program, so this works fine.
13  *
